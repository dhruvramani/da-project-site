Background

  • Currency Exchange - Currency exchange or Foreign exchange is termed as the conversion of one country’s currency into another and is dependent on a lot of economic factors such as trade, geopolitical stability, etc. It also gives a metric of the demand of that currency globally, i.e., it tells you how much of one currency you need to buy one unit of the other currency.
  • Previous Research - Work from Karakostas [2] presents a latest survey on the different research trends and previous research. This paper gave us the relevant background needed to understand the problem space. Kamruzzaman and Sarker’s [1] work on using Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) for forecasting the currency exchange presents some interesting metrics and simulation results.
