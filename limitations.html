Limitations of the Study

  • High volatility - Currency markets are very volatile and unpredictable due to various events or policies.
  • Depends on multiple factors - Exchange rate depends on a multitude of factors such as inflation, GDP, trade information etc.
  • Data Quality - Inaccuracies in data recording, missing data, credibility of sources etc are difficult to control.
  • Assumptions and model complexity - Due to multitude of factors, incorporating all the factors in a model accurately is very difficult and is an active area of research.
  • Lack of causality - Lot of factors show correlation with the foreign exchange rate data but it does not imply causality.
  • Cases of overfitting - Due to the complexity of the models, overfitting can be a real problem with increase in historical data.
